Figma

I utilize Figma for all of my prototpying needs. Wireframes, mockups, low and hi-fidelity ptototypes - Figma does it all! I enjoy using Figma as my priamry prototyping tool because it is currently up to industry standard and because the shortcuts are similar to Photoshop.

User Research

As a UX/UI Designer, it is important for me to never design just for the sake of desining. Design that is user-centered, based on collected and analyzed data, to me, produces the best results. I conduct user interviews and distribute surveys to analyze data that informs my UI.
